Beijing is a city of immense scale and history. 

Standing on the **Great Wall of China** at Mutianyu is a humbling experience. The wall snakes across the mountains as far as the eye can see, a testament to ancient engineering.

But Beijing isn't just about the past. Walking through the Sanlitun district at night reveals a modern, fast-paced metropolis filled with incredible dining and vibrant nightlife.

## Must-Do in Beijing
- Visit the Forbidden City.
- Eat authentic Peking Duck.
- Explore the historic Hutongs.
