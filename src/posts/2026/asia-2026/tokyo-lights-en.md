Japan is a masterclass in contrasts.

In Tokyo, the neon signs of Akihabara and the organized chaos of the Shibuya Crossing are mesmerizing. The energy is palpable, the food is unmatched, and every convenience store feels like a treasure trove.

But hop on the Shinkansen (bullet train) to Kyoto, and the world changes. The pace slows down. Walking through the bamboo forests of Arashiyama or visiting a traditional tea house offers a deep sense of peace.
