Welcome to Seoul, the bustling capital of South Korea! 

This city is an incredible blend of the deeply traditional and the ultra-modern. In the morning, you can wander through the peaceful grounds of **Gyeongbokgung Palace**, wearing a rented Hanbok, and by the afternoon, you're sipping a futuristic latte in a neon-lit cafe in *Gangnam*.

## Street Food Paradise
Myeongdong is a must-visit for street food lovers. From tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes) to hotteok (sweet pancakes), the flavors are bold and unforgettable. 

1. **Tteokbokki**: Spicy and chewy.
2. **Kimbap**: The perfect on-the-go meal.
3. **Korean Fried Chicken**: Crispy perfection.
