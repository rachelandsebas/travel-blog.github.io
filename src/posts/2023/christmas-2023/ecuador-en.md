### Años Viejos

In Ecuador on New Year's Eve, it's a tradition to buy a paper mache character which you then burn on a huge bonfire. The characters are called Años Viejos (meaning Old Year), and burning them represents getting rid of the bad things from the last year to start fresh in the new year. On one of our last days in Ecuador, we went downtown to see the Años Viejos!
