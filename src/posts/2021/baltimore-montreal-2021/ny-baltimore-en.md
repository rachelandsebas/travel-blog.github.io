Sebastián went to a Citadel event in New York City at the Rockefeller building. He flew into NYC the morning of August 6. Upon arrival, he headed to his AirBnb, which ended up being an empty lot. 

Confused and frustrated, he headed to a nearby Ecuadorian restaurant to figure out what to do. He ordered an humita, guatita, and jugo de maracuyá - which he still can remember off the top of his head, while writing this five years later. It had been much too long without Ecuadorian food (two years because of Covid!). The restaurant staff also helpfully pointed him to a nearby area with hotels, and he was able to get a room.

## Sebastián’s Birthday
After the end of the event on the second day, Rachel flew into New York. Sebastián met Rachel at the airport, and together we took the train to Baltimore to meet Rachel’s family.

We celebrated Sebastián’s birthday with Indian food for dinner. At dinner Sebastián also opened his gift from Rachel’s parents. They made him work for his gift by decoding a message - which said that the gift was a visit to an escape room.

A few days, we all did the escape room together. It was a pirate themed room, The Captain’s Bounty, and we made it out with 8 minutes and 40 seconds to spare. This was our first escape room - and our first win - of many, many more to come.

## Time in Baltimore
The two weeks we had in Baltimore were great, and very busy.

Sebastián was finishing up his final exams and projects, and then very shortly after that started working (remotely for coop).

Rachel spent much of the time visiting friends from high school.

We also, of course, played lots of board games, had good conversations, and just enjoyed time with family and friends. The evening before we left, we walked up to The Cow (an ice cream shop) and met up with a bunch of friends.

## New York
Arriving in New York, it was pouring rain. We managed to make it to the same Ecuadorian restaurant Sebastián had visited two weeks before, and Rachel got to try Ecuadorian food (humitas) for the first time. They were delicious!

We then spent the night at a friend’s apartment in New York, before catching an early morning flight to Montreal.
