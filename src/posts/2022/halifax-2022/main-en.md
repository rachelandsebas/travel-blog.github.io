My good friends and roommates, Claire and Elizabeth, found a great deal on flights, so we decided to take a trip! Reading week of my last term, we flew out to Halifax for a little trip to see Caroline!

## Kings College
Arriving in Halifax, we met up with Caroline, and got to see a bit of King’s College. Unfortunately, due to covid rules we couldn’t go very far inside, but it was lovely to meet some of Caroline’s friends.

## King’s Library and Public Gardens
On our first full day, we toured the King’s library, seeing many of its historical artifacts. Then, we went downtown and visited the Public Gardens and Citadel Hill.

## Peggy’s Cove
The next day, we rented a car and took a day trip to Peggy’s Cove, stopping at a few different places along the way. The views were gorgeous and the light was perfect for photos.

On the way back, we sang Hamilton karaoke in the car and stopped to try the famous Halifax donairs.

## Sister Time
The last full day in Halifax, Caroline and I spent the day hanging out, watching movies, and chatting together. That evening, all four of us had a movie night, watching Don’t Look Up.

Lots of fun memories, laughing and talking together.
