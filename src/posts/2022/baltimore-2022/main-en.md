### Christmas 2022 - The Journey

Sebastián and Rachel had flights booked for Christmas Eve, flying from Toronto to Baltimore and arriving that afternoon.

::: carousel
![First step: bus from Waterloo to Toronto](images/bus_1.jpg)
![The airport was packed as winter storms disrupted travel across Ontario and the eastern US](images/cancelled_2.jpg)
:::

When they arrived at the airport, they learned that their flight had been cancelled. Snowstorms were causing widespread delays and cancellations, and the phone lines for Air Canada were overwhelmed. After trying to make a plan, they found themselves stuck in a long line for airport rebooking help.

::: carousel
![The crowd at the airport](images/cancelled_1.jpg)
![The road closures made the drive look risky and unrealistic](images/driving_1.jpg)
:::

They explored every option: rental cars, other airports, and nearby cities, but conditions were too dangerous. With no good choice left, they finally accepted a later flight that would arrive in Baltimore after Christmas. The next morning, a new opportunity appeared, and they rushed to the airport again.

::: carousel
![The itinerary of the fake flight](images/fakeflight_1.jpg)
![A message from Dad showing the fake flight on its way](images/fakeflight_2.jpg)
:::

The plan was to surprise Rachel's family by pretending they were taking a different flight. They boarded the real plane, waited through a long delay, and eventually landed in Newark. From there, they took an Uber to the train station and made their way to Baltimore.

::: carousel
![Meanwhile: already on the plane](images/plane_1[77].jpg)
![Meanwhile: already in the US and heading to the station](images/uber_1.jpg)
:::

After a long and stressful journey, they arrived at the station and then the house, where the surprise finally unfolded. Rachel's family opened the door to a pair of very tired but very happy travelers.

![Just outside the station](images/station_2.jpg)
![The surprise at home](images/home_1.jpg)
:::
